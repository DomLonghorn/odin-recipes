One-Pan Puttanesca

Ingredients

  • 50 ml olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 400g Chickpeas
  • Lemon
  • Parsley

Cooking Instructions

  1. Fry the chickpeas with the spices
  2. Crush the garlic and add to the pan
  3. Chop the parsley
  4. Add stock to the pan and bring to the boil
  5. Add the pasta and cook until al dent (12 or so minutes)
  6. Stir in the parsley and lemon juice
  7. Season with salt and pepper
